Growing Guide

Follow these essential tips to ensure your dahlias thrive all summer long:

☀️ Site & Soil

Plant in full sun (6-8 hours daily) after the last frost when soil is at least 60°F. Ensure the soil is well-draining to prevent tuber rot.

🌱 Planting

Dig a hole 4-6 inches deep. Place the tuber horizontally with the eye facing up. Do not water immediately after planting until green shoots appear.

✂️ Maintenance

Pinch the center stem when the plant reaches 12 inches tall to encourage branching. Stake tall varieties early to prevent them from snapping under the weight of the blooms.

🌸 Deadheading

Regularly snip off spent blooms. This redirects the plant's energy into producing more beautiful flowers all season long.

❄️ Winter Storage

Dahlias are tender. After the first frost, carefully dig up the tubers, gently wash them, and let them dry. Store them in a dark location between 40°F and 50°F to keep them perfectly dormant over winter.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tuber sales and shipping.

📦 When will my tubers ship?

We begin shipping in mid-April once temperatures are safe. Please note: We currently only ship to the Continental USA (excluding Alaska and Hawaii).

✅ Do you guarantee your tubers?

Yes. We guarantee that all tubers shipped will have at least one visible eye. Customers must contact us within 48 hours of delivery for any questions or concerns.

🛍️ Can I add to my order after it's placed?

To ensure shipping efficiency, we cannot combine orders or add items once an order is finalized. Please double-check your cart before checking out!

🌡️ How should I store them if I'm not ready to plant?

If your tubers arrive before your soil is warm enough (60°F), store them in a cool, dark, and dry place (40-50°F). Do not refrigerate or allow them to freeze.
